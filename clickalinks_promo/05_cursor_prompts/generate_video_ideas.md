# Cursor Prompts for Generating Video Content Ideas

Use these prompts with Cursor AI (or similar AI tools) to brainstorm video content for ClickaLinks.

---

## Prompt 1: Generate Video Concept Ideas

```
Generate 10 creative video concept ideas for ClickaLinks advertising platform. 
Each idea should include:
- Video title
- Duration (15, 30, 60, or 90 seconds)
- Target platform (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn)
- Hook (first 3 seconds)
- Main message
- Visual style description
- Call-to-action

Focus on:
- Affordable advertising (£1 per day)
- Fair rotation system
- Easy setup process
- Target audience: small business owners, local businesses, online stores
- Brand colors: orange (#ff6b35), cyan (#06b6d4), blue (#3b82f6), green (#10b981), pink (#ec4899), yellow (#fbbf24)
- Tone: professional yet approachable, energetic, helpful
```

## Usage Tips:

1. **Customize Prompts:** Adjust based on your specific needs
2. **Iterate:** Refine ideas with follow-up prompts
3. **Combine:** Merge multiple concepts for comprehensive content
4. **Prioritize:** Focus on ideas that align with your goals
5. **Test:** Try different concepts and see what resonates
6. **Track:** Monitor performance and double down on what works
7. **Stay On-Brand:** Ensure all ideas match ClickaLinks voice and values
8. **Platform-Specific:** Adapt ideas for each platform's culture

## Content Pillars to Focus On:

1. **Affordability** - £1 per day messaging
2. **Fairness** - Equal visibility, rotation system
3. **Simplicity** - Easy setup, no contracts
4. **Results** - Real business growth stories
5. **Transparency** - No hidden fees, clear pricing
6. **Community** - Supporting small businesses
7. **Innovation** - Modern advertising platform
8. **Value** - Best ROI for small businesses

---

## Remember:

- **Hook First:** First 3 seconds determine engagement
- **Value Early:** State benefit quickly
- **Visual Storytelling:** Show, don't just tell
- **Clear CTA:** Make action obvious
- **Platform Native:** Adapt for each platform
- **Test & Learn:** Try variations and optimize
- **Stay Consistent:** Maintain brand voice and colors
- **Engage:** Encourage comments and shares
