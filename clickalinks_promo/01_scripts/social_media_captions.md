## Best Practices:

1. **Hook First:** First line determines engagement
2. **Value Early:** State benefit within first 3 lines
3. **Visual Breaks:** Use emojis, line breaks for scannability
4. **Clear CTA:** Make action obvious
5. **Authentic:** Sound human, not robotic
6. **Platform-Specific:** Adapt length and tone
7. **Engage:** Ask questions, encourage comments
8. **Test:** Try variations and track what works
