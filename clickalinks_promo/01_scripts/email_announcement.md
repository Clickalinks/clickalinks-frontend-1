## Email Best Practices:

1. **Personalization:** Use [Name] placeholder
2. **Clear CTAs:** Make action items obvious
3. **Mobile-Friendly:** Keep paragraphs short
4. **Brand Colors:** Use brand gradient in header/footer
5. **Links:** Make all links trackable
6. **Unsubscribe:** Always include unsubscribe option
