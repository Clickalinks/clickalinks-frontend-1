# Hero Launch Video - Voiceover Script

**Duration:** 30-45 seconds  
**Tone:** Energetic, Professional, Exciting  
**Target Audience:** Business owners looking for affordable advertising

---

## Script:

**[0:00-0:05] Opening Hook**
"Tired of expensive advertising that doesn't deliver? Introducing ClickaLinks - the UK's most affordable advertising platform."

**[0:05-0:15] Problem & Solution**
"For just £1 per day, get your business in front of thousands of engaged customers. No complicated contracts, no hidden fees, no minimum spend."

**[0:15-0:25] Key Benefits**
"Choose your spot, set your campaign duration, and watch your business grow. With automatic rotation, every advertiser gets fair visibility."

**[0:25-0:35] Call to Action**
"Join hundreds of businesses already advertising on ClickaLinks. Start your campaign today at clickalinks.com"

**[0:35-0:40] Closing**
"Clickalinks - Where deals meet customers. Get started now!"

---

## Alternative Shorter Version (15 seconds):

"Get your business seen for just £1 per day. ClickaLinks connects your deals with customers actively looking to save. Choose your spot, set your duration, and go live instantly. Join hundreds of businesses advertising on ClickaLinks. Visit clickalinks.com today!"

---

## Voiceover Tips:

- **Pacing:** Medium-fast, energetic but clear
- **Emphasis:** "£1 per day", "clickalinks.com", "Get started now"
- **Tone:** Confident, friendly, professional
- **Background Music:** Upbeat, corporate-friendly, fade during voiceover
- **Visual Sync:** Match key phrases with on-screen text/animations

---

## Visual Cues (for video editor):

- [0:00] Logo reveal with brand colors
- [0:05] Grid animation showing available spots
- [0:15] Stats overlay: "£1/day", "2,000 spots", "10 pages"
- [0:25] CTA button animation
- [0:35] Website URL with QR code
