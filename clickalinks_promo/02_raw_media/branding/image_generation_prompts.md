## General Tips for Image Generation:

1. **Always specify dimensions** - Important for social media
2. **Include brand colors** - Use hex codes when possible
3. **Mention style** - "Modern", "Professional", "Vibrant"
4. **Specify text** - If you want specific text included
5. **Quality level** - "High quality", "4K", "Professional"
6. **Iterate** - Refine prompts based on results
7. **Test variations** - Try different angles/styles
8. **Brand consistency** - Keep colors and style consistent
