## General Video Editing Tips:

### Transitions:
- Use brand colors in transitions
- Smooth cuts for professional videos
- Trendy transitions for social media (zoom, spin, glitch)
- Match transition style to music

### Text Overlays:
- Use brand gradient for text
- Bold, readable fonts
- Animated entrances (fade, slide, scale)
- Keep on screen long enough to read (3-5 seconds)
- High contrast for readability

### Music:
- Energetic for promotional videos
- Corporate-friendly for B2B
- Match tempo to video pace
- Fade during voiceover
- Royalty-free music only

### Screen Recordings:
- Record in 1080p minimum
- Use zoom/pan for interest
- Highlight important areas
- Speed up boring parts (2x-3x)
- Add cursor highlights if needed

### Color Grading:
- Enhance brand colors
- Consistent look across all videos
- Professional, vibrant
- High contrast

### Export Settings:
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080) for horizontal
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 for vertical (TikTok/Reels)
- **Format:** MP4
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Bitrate:** High (for quality)
